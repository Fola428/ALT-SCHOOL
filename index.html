Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

This hearty Italian classic is perfect for a family dinner. Packed with rich flavors, slow-cooked for tenderness, and absolutely delicious.

Recipe Information

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Cooking time: 1 hour

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 400g spaghetti

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and garlic, cook until softened.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  7. Drain spaghetti, then serve topped with the sauce.

Tips

For extra flavor, add a splash of red wine when cooking the sauce. You can also sprinkle freshly grated Parmesan cheese on top before serving.
